Our History & Core Values

CTY International School is a nursery and primary school which was estalished to meet the yearning of Muslim elites who want a school that provides opportunity for their young ones to memorize Qur’an as well as acquire qualitative western education. The school is an off – school of a monthly Islamic children programme called catch Then Young organized by Averroes Educational Institute.

The school started on the 18th of September year 2000 with a student population of 32 spread on the three classes Twenty-five (25) students are in boarding house while 7 of them are day students.
